Who We Are

About LabConnect Home

LabConnect Home was founded with one simple belief — your health should never wait. We are a Delhi-based home diagnostic service built for families, working professionals, senior citizens, and anyone who deserves quality healthcare without the hassle of stepping outside.
Our team of trained and certified phlebotomists visits your home at your preferred time, collects samples with clinical precision using sterile single-use equipment, and delivers your digital reports directly on WhatsApp — all within 24 hours.
We believe diagnostics should be affordable, accessible, and trustworthy. That is why we keep our prices transparent, our process simple, and our service genuinely personal. No long queues. No confusion. Just care — at your door.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions About Home Blood Tests in Delhi

Where do you offer blood test at home in Delhi?

+
LabConnect Home provides blood test at home in West Delhi including Vikaspuri, Uttam Nagar, Dwarka, Janakpuri, Hastal Road and Nawada. We are rapidly expanding to cover all of Delhi NCR. Call +91 92171 21345 to confirm your area and book same-day collection.

What is the cost of CBC blood test at home in Delhi?

+
Our CBC (Complete Blood Count) blood test at home in Delhi costs just ₹300. This price includes free home collection by a certified phlebotomist and WhatsApp report delivery within 24 hours. No hidden charges.

What is the cheapest home blood test available in Delhi?

+
Blood Sugar (Fasting + PP) test is available at just ₹100 with free home sample collection anywhere in West Delhi. Other affordable tests: Urine Routine ₹250, KFT ₹400, HbA1c ₹500. Wellness packages start at ₹999.

How quickly will I receive my lab reports?

+
All test reports are delivered digitally on WhatsApp within 24 hours of sample collection. Urine Routine reports arrive within 12 hours. We don't keep you waiting.

Is your home sample collection service safe and hygienic?

+
Absolutely. All our phlebotomists are trained and certified. We use sterile, single-use equipment for every collection. Gloves, masks, and proper disposal protocols are followed at all times. Your safety is our top priority.

Do you offer antenatal blood test packages at home?

+
Yes! Our Antenatal Care Profile at ₹1,999 is designed for expecting mothers. It covers CBC, Blood Group & Rh, Blood Sugar, Urine Routine, HIV, HBsAg (Hepatitis B), VDRL/Syphilis, Thyroid Profile, Iron Profile, Vitamin B12 and Vitamin D — all collected safely at your home.

How do I book a blood test at home in Delhi?

+
Booking takes under 2 minutes. You can: WhatsApp us at +91 92171 21345, call us directly, or fill the booking form on this page. We confirm your slot within 30 minutes and send a reminder before our team arrives.

What payment methods do you accept?

+
We accept Cash, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), and online bank transfer. Payment is collected after the sample is collected — no advance required for most tests.
